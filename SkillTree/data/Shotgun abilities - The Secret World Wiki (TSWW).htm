Tier One Skills

Tier One skills are available immediately to the player upon starting the game, and can spend AP in either of the two Shotgun blocks without unlocking the other first.

Unlocking all of the Tier One skills rewards the character with the Enforce and Control Achievement.

Enforce

  • Pump Action - [1 AP, Active, Cone AoE]
    A shotgun attack that deals a moderate amount of damage in a cone in front of you. Builds a weapon resource for each equipped weapon.
  • Sawed Off - [1 AP, Passive, Modifies: Pump Action]
    Each successful hit with Pump Action reduces the target's damage output for a short time. Sets weakened state.
  • Out For A Kill - [1 AP, Active, Single]
    A single target shotgun attack that deals a large amount of damage. Consumes all shotgun resources
  • Dead On Target - [2 AP, Passive]
    Increases all damage dealt by shotgun abilities.
  • Buckshot - [3 AP, Active, Single]
    A single target shotgun attack that deals a moderate amount of damage. Deals more damage to targets with a full set of shotgun resources on them.
  • Rapid Reload - [4 AP, Passive, Modifies: Buckshot]
    Reduces the activation time of Buckshot.
  • Kneecapper - [7 AP, Elite Active, Cone AoE]
    A shotgun attack that deals a moderate amount of damage and slows enemies in a cone in front of you. Sets hindered state.

Control

  • Hit and Run - [1 AP, Passive]
    If a target dies with your shotgun resources on it, it will explode and deal damage to nearby enemies.
  • Powder Burn - [1 AP, Active, Strike, Single]
    A single target shotgun strike that deals a moderate amount of damage. Exploits hindered state. Builds a weapon resource for each equipped weapon.
  • Gunsmoke - [1 AP, Passive]
    Whenever you set weakened state, you also deal a small amount of damage.
  • Breaching Shot - [2 AP, Active, Group]
    Increases the penetration chance of everyone in your group for a short time.
  • Breach Party - [3 AP, Passive, Modifies: Breaching Shot]
    Reduces the recharge time of Breaching Shot.
  • Both Barrels - [4 AP, Active, Blast, Cone AoE]
    A shotgun blast that deals a large amount of damage in a cone in front of you. Consumes all shotgun resources.
  • Authority (Ability) - [7 AP, Elite Passive]
    Increases the efficiency of all your group-wide effects.

Tier Two Skills

To unlock the Tier Two skill clusters, the player must unlock all of the skills in the Tier One Shotgun clusters.

Crackdown

  • Fire and Forget - [9 AP, Active, Blast, Column AoE]
    A shotgun blast that deals a moderate amount of damage in a column in front of you. Builds a weapon resource for each equipped weapon.
  • 12 Gouge - [12 AP, Passive]
    Whenever you Penetrate, you reduce your target's damage output for a short time. Sets weakened state.
  • Riot Act - [16 AP, Active, TAoE]
    A shotgun attack that deals a large amount of damage to your target and nearby enemies. Consumes all shotgun resources.
  • Riot Control - [21 AP, Passive, Modifies: Riot Act]
    Riot Act also leaves an area on the ground that reduces affected targets' movement speed for a short time.
  • Stopping Power - [27 AP, Active, Cone AoE]
    A shotgun attack that deals a large amount of damage in a cone in front of you. Exploits hindered state. Consumes all shotgun resources.
  • Blast Corps - [34 AP, Passive]
    All your blast category attacks add a small damage component to the consecutive attack.
  • Shotgun Wedding - [50 AP, Elite Active, Cone AoE]
    A shotgun channel that deals a large amount of damage in a cone in front of you, over multiple hits, for each successful hit, the next hit will deal increased damage.

Restricted Access

  • Beanbag Rounds - [9 AP, Passive]
    Whenever you set hindered state, your penetration chance is increased for a short time.
  • Kickback - [12 AP, Active, Dash, Single/Column AoE]
    You fire at your target and dash backwards, dealing moderate damage in a column behind you.
  • Pinned Down - [16 AP, Passive]
    Whenever you penetrate, slow the target. Sets hindered state.
  • Takedown - [21 AP, Active, Single]
    A single target shotgun attack that deals a large amount of damage and slows the target. Sets hindered state.
  • Overpenetration - [27 AP, Passive]
    Every fifth time you penetrate, you get an increase in penetration chance for a short time.
  • Lock, Stock, and Barrel - [34 AP, Active, Self]
    A shotgun ability that, whenever activated, will make your next shotgun resource consumer instantly build five new shotgun resources.
  • Close Quarters - [50 AP, Elite Passive]
    Whenever you hit a target in very short range, that target is slowed. Sets hindered state.

Close Encounters

  • Single Barrel - [9 AP, Active, Single]
    A single target shotgun attack that deals a moderate amount of damage. Exploits weakened state. Builds a weapon resource for each equipped weapon.
  • Strike Force - [12 AP, Passive]
    Increases the penetration chance of all your strike category abilities.
  • Raging Bullet - [16 AP, Active, Strike, Single]
    A single target shotgun strike that deals a large amount of damage. Exploits close range. Consumes all shotgun resources.
  • Body Piercing - [21 AP, Passive]
    Whenever you penetrate, you get an increased penetration chance for a short time.
  • Heavy Recoil - [27 AP, Active, Dash, Single]
    A single target shotgun attack that deals a large amount of damage. Exploits weakened state. Consumes all shotgun resources.
  • Invasive Measures - [34 AP, Passive]
    Whenever you hit a weakened target, you have a chance to build a weapon resource for the weapon that activated Invasive Measures.
  • Point Blank - [50 AP, Elite Active, Single]
    A single target shotgun attack that deals a large amount of damage. Exploits close range. Conditional knockback.

Securing the Perimeter

  • Scattershot - [9 AP, Active, TAoE]
    A shotgun attack that deals a moderate amount of damage to your target and enemies close to your target. Builds a weapons resource for all equipped weapons.
  • Collateral Damage - [12 AP, Passive]
    All your blast category attacks have a chance of purging a beneficial effect from your target.
  • Shotgun Turret - [16 AP, Active, Blast/Summon, PBAoE]
    A shotgun blast attack that places a turret at your feet that will attack nearby enemies for a short time. Consumes all shotgun resources.
  • High-Gauge Turret - [21 AP, Passive, Modifies: Shotgun Turret]
    Increases the damage of Shotgun Turret.
  • Tear Gas - [27 AP, Active, TAoE]
    A shotgun attack that deals a large amount of damage to your target and enemies close to your target. Exploits hindered state. Consumes all shotgun resources.
  • Mitigating Factor - [34 AP, Passive]
    Whenever you penetrate, your mitigation is increased for a short time.
  • Cleanup - [50 AP, Elite Active, Single/Group, Effect: Cleanse]
    A shotgun attack that, while active, will cleanse detrimental effects from everyone in your group, and purge beneficial effects from their targets.

Hunkering Down

  • Crap Shoot - [9 AP, Passive]
    Whenever you hit a Weakened target, there is a chance you will purge a beneficial effect from it.
  • Smart Bomb - [12 AP, Active, GTAoE, Effect: Purge]
    Shotgun attack that places a mine at the targeted point that will explode and deal a small amount of damage. Purges beneficial effects from enemies caught in the blast.
  • Hardcase - [16 AP, Passive]
    Whenever you set weakened state, your mitigation is increased for a short time.
  • Flak Jacket - [21 AP, Active, Group]
    Significantly reduces the damage taken by your entire group for a period of time.
  • Full Flak Jacket - [27 AP, Passive, Modifies: Flak Jacket]
    Increases the duration of Flak Jacket.
  • Bomb Squad - [34 AP, Active, GTAoE]
    Shotgun attack that places a mine at the targeted point that will explode and deal a large amount of damage to enemies caught in the blast. Sets hindered state.
  • Shield Formation - [50 AP, Elite Passive]
    Whenever you gain mitigation bonuses, there is a chance the effects will also affect nearby group members.

Tactical Surprise

  • Striker - [9 AP, Active, Single]
    A single-target shotgun attack that deals moderate damage. Builds a weapon resource for both equipped weapons.
  • Impact Striker - [12 AP, Passive, Modifies: Striker]
    Significantly increases damage Striker deals to Hindered targets.
  • Defensive Turret - [16 AP, Active, Summon]
    A shotgun attack that places a turret at your feet that decreases the damage dealt to nearby friendly targets for a short time. Consumes all shotgun resources.
  • Punisher - [21 AP, Passive]
    All your strike category attacks also reduce your target's damage output for a short time. Sets weakened state.
  • Sure Shot - [27 AP, Active, Single]
    A single-target shotgun attack that deals a large amount of damage. Exploits hindered state. Consumes all shotgun resources.
  • Perjury - [34 AP, Passive]
    Whenever you set hindered state, you all cleanse a detrimental effect from yourself
  • Rail Turret - [50 AP, Elite Active, Summon, Single]
    A shotgun attack that places a turret at your feet for a short time that will deal a large amount of damage to the closest target.
