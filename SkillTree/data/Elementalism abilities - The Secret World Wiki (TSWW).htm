Tier One Abilities

Tier One Elementalism abilities are available to the player upon starting The Secret World. Purchasing all inner wheel x abilities awards the Spark & React Achievement.

Spark

  • Shock - [1 AP, Active, Single]
    A single target elementalism attack that deals a moderate amount of damage. Builds a weapon resource for each equipped weapon.
  • Volatile Current - [1 AP, Passive, Modifies: Shock]
    Shock also deals damage over time. Sets afflicted state.
  • Combust - [1 AP, Active, Single]
    A single target elementalism attack that deals a large amount of damage. Consumes two elemental resources, or one elemental resource if the target is hindered.
  • Rapid Combustion - [2 AP, Passive, Modifies: Combust]
    Reduces the activation time of Combust.
  • Electrical Storm - [3 AP, Active, Chain, TAoE]
    An elementalism chain that deals a large amount of damage to the target before jumping to nearby targets. Consumes three elemental resources.
  • Elemental Precision - [4 AP, Passive]
    Increases damage dealt by Elementalism abilities.
  • Molten Earth - [7 AP, Elite Active, Single]
    A single target elementalism attack that deals a moderate amount of damage and rootes the target. Sets hindered state.

React

  • Mind over Matter - [1 AP, Passive]
    Whenever you critically hit, you also deal a small amount of damage over time. Sets afflicted state.
  • Ignite - [1 AP, Active, Single]
    A single target elementalism attack that deals a moderate amount of damage. Does more damage if you have no elemental resources. Builds a weapon resource for each equipped weapon.
  • Elemental Force - [1 AP, Passive]
    Every eighth attack you unleash is guaranteed to critically hit.
  • Anima Charge - [2 AP, Active, Self]
    Whenever you activate Anima Charge, your next elementalism consumer will consume no resources.
  • High Voltage - [3 AP, Passive, Modifies: Anima Charge]
    Whenever you activate Anima Charge, the affected consumer is also guaranteed to critically hit.
  • Thor's Hammer - [4 AP, Active, Strike, Single]
    A violent single target elementalism strike that deals a large amount of damage. Consumes five elemental resources.
  • Toxic Earth - [7 AP, Elite Passive]
    Whenever you hinder a target, you also deal a small amount of damage over time. Sets afflicted state.

Tier Two Abilities

The second tier of Elementalism abilities can only be accessed once the player has unlocked all of the abilities from both Spark and React.

Zero Crossing

  • Electrical Bolt - [9 AP, Active, Single]
    A single target elementalism attack that deals a moderate amount of damage. Exploits hindered state. Builds a weapon resource for each equipped weapon.
  • High Boltage - [12 AP, Passive, Modifies: Electrical Bolt]
    Increases the critical chance of Electrical Bolt.
  • Flame Strike - [16 AP, Active, Strike, Single]
    A single target elementalism strike that deals a large amount of damage. Consumes two elemental resources.
  • Violent Strikes - [21 AP, Passive]
    Significantly increases the critical hit chance of all your strike category abilities.
  • Blaze - [27 AP, Active, Single]
    An elementalism attack that deals a large amount of damage to your target. Exploits afflicted state. Consumes three elemental resources.
  • Aidelon - [34 AP, Passive, Modifies: Blaze]
    Blaze will always critically hit targets in afflicted state.
  • Power Line - [50 AP, Elite Active, Single]
    Attaches a power line to your target for a short time that deals damage in multiple hits. Conditional root.

Altered States

  • Molecular Exploitation - [9 AP, Passive]
    Whenever you hit a target in an afflicted state, you deal an extra hit for a small amount of damage.
  • Coldwave - [12 AP, Active, PBAoE]
    An elementalism attack that deals a moderate amount of damage and roots nearby enemies. Sets hindred state.
  • Criticality - [16 AP, Passive]
    Whenever you critically hit a target in afflicted state, you trigger a chain attack that will deal a moderate amount of damage to nearby enemies.
  • Flicker - [21 AP, Active, Self]
    You teleport forward, cleansing all hindered effects from yourself.
  • Highly Strung - [27 AP, Passive]
    Whenever you deplete all your elemental resources, you will deal additional damage for a short time.
  • Flashpoint - [34 AP, Active, Single]
    A single target elementalism attack that deals a small amount of damage and damage over time. Sets afflicted state. Conditional effects.
  • Live Wire - [50 AP, Elite Passive]
    Every fifth critical hit will trigger an additional hit that deals a large amount of damage to the target.

Mortality Curve

  • Arc Flash - [9 AP, Active, Chain, TAoE]
    An elementalism chain that deals a moderate amount of damage to your target and jumps to nearby enemies. Builds a weapon resource for each equipped weapon.
  • Death Density - [12 AP, Passive]
    Whenever you kill an enemy, your critical hit chance is increased for a short time.
  • Inferno - [16 AP, Active, GTAoE]
    An elementalism attack that deals a large amount of damage to your target and nearby enemies. Consumes five elemental resources.
  • Purgatory - [21 AP, Passive, Modifies: Inferno]
    Inferno also leaves an area on the ground for a short time that deals damage to enemies inside the area.
  • Conditional Force - [27 AP, Active, Chain, TAoE]
    An elementalism chain that deals a moderate amount of damage to your target and jumps to nearby enemies. Consumes two elemental resources. Exploits hindered state.
  • Chain Reaction - [34 AP, Passive]
    Whenever you successfully hit with a chain category ability, you also apply a small amount of damage over time. Sets afflicted state.
  • Whiteout - [50 AP, Elite Active, GTAoE]
    A ground targeted elementalism attack that deals damage and roots enemies inside the targeted area. Sets hindered state.

Resonance

  • Ignition - [9 AP, Active, Strike, Single]
    An elementalism strike that deals a moderate amount of damage. Builds a weapon resource for each equipped weapon.
  • Dial Down - [12 AP, Passive]
    Whenever you set hindered state, you also purge a beneficial effect.
  • Fire Manifestation - [16 AP, Active, Summon, GTAoE]
    Places a fire manifestation at the targeted point that will deal damage to nearby enemies for a short time. Consumes two elemental resources.
  • Social Dynamo - [21 AP, Passive]
    Whenever you critically hit with a strike category ability, all the members of your group have an increased chance to critically hit for a short time.
  • Magnetic Wipe - [27 AP, Active, Single, Effect: Purge]
    A single target elementalism attack that deals a moderate amount of damage and purges a beneficial effect. Exploits afflicted state. Consumes three elemental resources.
  • Magnetic Variance - [34 AP, Passive, Effect: Purge]
    All your critical hits have a chance to purge a beneficial effect from the target.
  • Hard Reset - [50 AP, Elite Active, Single, Effect: Purge]
    A single target attack that deals a moderate amount of damage and purges all beneficial effects from the target. Continues to purge the target for a short time.

Disturbance

  • Critical Control - [9 AP, Passive]
    Whenever you hit a target in hindered state, all your group members have an increases hit chance for a short time.
  • Electromagnetic Manifestation - [12 AP, Active, Summon, GTAoE, Effect: Purge]
    Places a manifestation at the targeted point that will deal a small amount of damage and purge beneficial effects from nearby enemies.
  • Damage Control - [16 AP, Passive]
    Whenever you critically hit a hindered target, you deal an extra hit for a small amount of damage.
  • Short Fuse - [21 AP, Active, Group]
    Increases the damage output of everyone in your group for a short time.
  • Final Fuse - [27 AP, Passive, Modifies: Short Fuse]
    Increases the damage increases granted by Short Fuse.
  • Ice Manifestation - [34 AP, Active, Summon, TAoE]
    Places an ice manifestation at the targeted point that deals damage and roots nearby enemies. Sets hindered state.
  • Big Bang - [50 AP, Elite Passive]
    All your manifestation, turret and drones' abilities hit nearby enemies for extra damage.

Tempest

  • Wildfire - [9 AP, Active, TAoE]
    An elementalism attack that deals a moderate amount of damage to your target and nearby enemies. Builds a weapon resource for each equipped weapon.
  • Phantom Purge - [12 AP, Passive]
    All your chain category attacks have a chance to purge a beneficial effect from the target.
  • Lightning Manifestation - [16 AP, Active, Summon/Chain, TAoE]
    Places a lightning manifestation at the targeted point that deals a moderate amount of damage to nearby targets. Exploits hindered state. Consumes 2 elemental resources.
  • Grounded - [21 AP, Passive]
    Whenever you purge, you deal an additional hit for a small amount of damage.
  • Burnout - [27 AP, Active, GTAoE]
    An elementalism attack that deals a large amount of damage and purges two beneficial effects from the target and nearby enemies. Consumes five elemental resources.
  • Lightning in a Bottle - [34 AP, Passive, Effect: Purge, Modifies: Lightning Manifestation]
    Lightning Manifestation also purges one beneficial effect from affected targets.
  • Overload - [50 AP, Elite Active, PBAoE]
    A channelled elementalism attack that deals a large amount of damage to nearby enemies over multiple hits. The last hit will root affected targets. Sets hindered state.
