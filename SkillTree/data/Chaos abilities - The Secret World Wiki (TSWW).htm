Tier One Abilities

Tier One Chaos abilities are available to the player upon starting The Secret World. Purchasing all inner wheel x abilities awards the Theory & Chance Achievement.

Theory

  • Hand of Change - [1 AP, Active, Single]
    A single target chaos attack that deals a moderate amount of damage. Builds a weapon resource for each equipped weapon.
  • Entropy (Ability) - [1 AP, Passive, Modifies: Hand of Change]
    Hand of Change builds an additional chaos resource every time it critically hits or penetrates.
  • Call for Eris - [1 AP, Active, Burst, Single]
    A single target chaos burst that deals a large amount of damage over multiple hits. Consumes all chaos resources.
  • Paradigm Shift - [2 AP, Passive]
    Whenever you set weakened state, you increase your hit chance for a short time.
  • Schism - [3 AP, Active, Single]
    A single target chaos attack that deals a large amount of damage and consumes all chaos resources. Deals additional damage to targets on low health.
  • Chaos Adept - [4 AP, Passive]
    Increases the damage of all chaos abilities.
  • Domino Effect - [7 AP, Elite Active, PBAoE]
    A powerful chaos attack that deals a moderate amount of damage to nearby enemies and pulls them towards you. Sets impaired state.

Chance

  • Gnosis - [1 AP, Passive]
    You have a chance to deal an extra hit when successfully hitting a target in weakened state.
  • Escalation - [1 AP, Active, Frenzy, PBAoE]
    A chaos frenzy that deals a moderate amount of damage to nearby enemies. Generates a large amount of hate. Sets weakened state. Builds a weapon resource for each equipped weapon.
  • Intensity - [1 AP, Passive, Effect: Minor Ward, Modifies: Escalation]
    Whenever you successfully hit a target in weakened state with Escalation, your mitigation is increased for a short time.
  • Illusion - [2 AP, Active, Effect: Major Evade Chance]
    Greatly increases your evade chance for a short time.
  • Master of Illusions - [3 AP, Passive, Modifies: Illusion]
    Further increases the evade chance granted by Illusion.
  • Paradox - [4 AP, Active, Single]
    A single target chaos attack that deals a moderate amount of damage. Exploits impaired state.
  • Probability - [7 AP, Elite Passive, Effect: Minor Ward]
    Whenever you evade an attack, you mitigation is increased for a short time, and whenever you land three hits in a row your damage output is increased for a short time.

Tier Two Abilities

The second tier of Chaos abilities can only be accessed once the player has unlocked all of the abilities from both Theory and Chance.

Teorema

  • Deconstruct - [9 AP, Active, Single]
    A single target chaos attack that deals a moderate amount of damage. Builds an extra chaos resource on targets of low health. Builds a weapon resource for each equipped weapon.
  • Cutting Thoughts - [12 AP, Passive]
    Every fifth successful hit will deal an additional three hits for a small amount of damage.
  • Four Horsemen - [16 AP, Active, Burst, Single]
    A single target chaos burst that deals a large amount of damage over multiple hits. Each successful hit in the sequence will increase in damage. Consumes all chaos resources.
  • Ripple Effect - [21 AP, Passive]
    Whenever you successfully connect two hits in a row with a burst category ability, you deal an additional hit for a small amount of damage.
  • Pulling the Strings - [27 AP, Active, Single]
    A single target chaos attack that deals a large amount of damage. Exploits weakened state. Consumes all chaos resources.
  • Pale Horse - [34 AP, Passive, Modifies: Pulling the Strings]
    Pulling the Strings deals significantly more damage to targets on low health.
  • Prisoner of Fate - [50 AP, Elite Active, Single]
    A channelled chaos attack that deals a large amount of damage over multiple hits. Deals increased damage to targets with low health. Sets impaired state.

The Value of x

  • Breakdown - [9 AP, Passive]
    Whenever you successfully hit a target with a Chaos, Blade or Hammer Ability , you increase all damage done to that target for a short time. Sets weakened state.
  • Amor Fati - [12 AP, Active]
    Increases your damage for a short time. While Amor Fati is active, your attacks cannot glance.
  • Pressure Point - [16 AP, Passive]
    Every eighth attack you unleash will knock the target to the ground for a short time. Sets impaired state.
  • Helter Skelter - [21 AP, Active, Dash]
    You dash forward and deal a small amount of damage in a column in front of you. Sets impaired state.
  • Pattern Recognition - [27 AP, Passive]
    Whenever you successfully hit an impaired target, you deal an extra hit for a small amount of damage, with a chance of a second extra hit.
  • Discord Rising - [34 AP, Active, PBAoE]
    Creates an area at your feet that moves forward dealing a moderate amount of damage to all enemies caught inside the area. Sets weakened state.
  • From the Abyss - [50 AP, Elite Passive]
    Every sixth successful attack will trigger an extra attack that deals a large amount of damage to nearby enemies. Deals more damage to targets on low health

Collapse

  • Wheel of Knives - [9 AP, Active, Frenzy, PBAoE]
    A chaos frenzy that deals a moderate amount of damage to nearby enemies. Builds a weapon resource for each equipped weapon.
  • Wheel of Misfortune - [12 AP, Passive, Modifies: Wheel of Knives]
    Wheel of Knives deals an extra hit to targets in impaired and weakened states.
  • Maelstrom - [16 AP, Active, PBAoE]
    A chaos attack that deals a large amount of damage to nearby enemies. Consumes all chaos resources.
  • Hysteria - [21 AP, Passive]
    Whenever you successfully hit with a frenzy category attack, your hit chance is increased for a short time.
  • Cry Havoc - [27 AP, Active, TAoE]
    A chaos attack that deals a large amount of damage to your target and nearby enemies. Exploits impaired state.
  • Sick Society - [34 AP, Passive]
    Whenever you hit a target on low health, you deal an extra hit for a small amount of damage.
  • Black Hole - [50 AP, Elite Active, Single]
    A single target that deals damage and roots the target to the ground. Nearby enemies must stay close or take damage and be pulled back towards the target. Sets hindered state.

Building Blocks

  • Run Rampant - [9 AP, Active, Burst, Single]
    A single target chaos burst that deals a moderate amount of damage over multiple hits and increases your evade chance. Builds a weapon resource for each equipped weapon.
  • Inevitability - [12 AP, Passive]
    Whenever you are attacked by a target in the weakened state, there's a chance your evade chance is increased.
  • Consequence - [16 AP, Active]
    Decreases incoming damage for a short time and the next hit you receive will deal damage back to the attacker and set weakened state. Consumes all chaos resources.
  • Burst of Energy - [21 AP, Passive, Effect: Minor Ward]
    All your burst category abilities increase your mitigation for a short time.
  • Suffering and Solace - [27 AP, Active, Single]
    A single target chaos attack that deals a large amount of damage. Exploits impaired state. Consumes all chaos resources.
  • Fever Pitch - [34 AP, Passive]
    Whenever you hit a target in weakened state, there's a chance you will get significantly increased hit chance.
  • Immutable - [50 AP, Elite Active]
    You are immune to defeat for a short time. If you are on low health when Immutable expires, you will be healed for a small amount.

Shell Game

  • Fixation - [9 AP, Passive]
    Whenever you hit target in weakened state, there's a chance your hit chance is improved for a short time.
  • Chaotic Pull - [12 AP, Active, Single]
    A single target, ranged chaos attack that deals a moderate amount of damage and pulls the target to you.
  • Contradiction - [16 AP, Passive]
    Whenever you evade an attack, you increase all damage dealt to that target for a short time. Sets weakened state.
  • Uneven Ground - [21 AP, Active]
    A chaos attack that deals a small amount of damage to nearby enemies. Sets weakened state.
  • Cause and Effect - [27 AP, Passive]
    Whenever you set impaired state, your next attack deals increased damage and generates increased hate.
  • Smoke and Mirrors - [34 AP, Active]
    Greatly increases your evade chance for a short time.
  • Prodigal - [50 AP, Elite Passive]
    Increases your maximum health and decreases the duration of all crowd control effects on you.

The Fourth Wall

  • Shaken Foundations - [9 AP, Active, TAoE]
    A chaos attack that deals a moderate amount of damage to your target and nearby enemies. Builds a weapon resource for each equipped weapon.
  • Backlash - [12 AP, Passive]
    Every seventh time you evade an attack, you deal a small amount of damage to nearby enemies
  • Karma - [16 AP, Active, Frenzy, PBAoE]
    A chaos frenzy that deals a large amount of damage to nearby enemies and cause them to heal the next target they hit. Consumes all chaos resources.
  • Turbulence - [21 AP, Passive, Effect: Evade Rating]
    Whenever you activate a Frenzy category ability, your evade chance is increased for a short time.
  • Reality Fracture - [27 AP, Active, GTAoE]
    A chaos attack that pulses for a short time dealing damage to nearby enemies and generating a large amount of hate. Exploits weakened state. Consumes all chaos resources.
  • Extended Fracture - [34 AP, Passive, Modifies: Reality Fracture]
    Increases the amount of damage dealt by Reality Fracture, and the amount of enemies affected.
  • Strange Attractor - [50 AP, Elite Active, PBAoE]
    A channelled chaos attack that deals a large amount of damage to nearby enemies and pulls affected targets to you.
