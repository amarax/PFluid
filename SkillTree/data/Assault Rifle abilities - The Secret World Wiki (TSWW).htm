Tier One Abilities

Tier One Assault Rifle abilities are available to the player upon starting The Secret World. Completing both Ability clusters awards the Engage and Support Achievement.

Engage

  • Safety Off - [1 AP, Active, Burst, Single]
    A single target assault rifle burst that deals a moderate amount of damage over multiple shots. Builds a weapon resource for each equipped weapon.
  • Extra Bullet - [1 AP, Passive, Modifies: Safety Off]
    Adds a shot to Safety Off's burst.
  • Fire at Will - [1 AP, Active, Frenzy, TAoE]
    An assault rifle frenzy that deals a large amount of damage in a targeted area. Consumes all assault rifle resources.
  • No Contest - [2 AP, Passive]
    Whenever you set hindered state, your next attack cannot be evaded.
  • Trigger Happy - [3 AP, Active, Single]
    A single target assault rifle attack that deals a moderate amount of damage. Exploits hindered state. Builds a weapon resource for each equipped weapon.
  • Eagle Eye - [4 AP, Passive]
    Increases all damage dealt by assault rifle abilities.
  • Slow the Advance - [7 AP, Elite Active, TAoE]
    An assault rifle attack that deals damage in a targeted area. Slows the movement speed of any targets hit. Sets hindered state.

Support

  • Military Code - [1 AP, Passive]
    If a target with your assault rifle resources on it dies, your next resource building ability will build an additional assault rifle resource.
  • Anima Shot - [1 AP, Active, Single, Effect: Leech]
    A single target assault rifle attack that deals damage to your offensive target and leeches health from your offensive to your defensive target. Builds one assault rifle resource.
  • Anima Boost - [1 AP, Passive, Modifies: Anima Shot]
    Increases both the damage and leech components of Anima Shot.
  • Lock & Load - [2 AP, Active]
    Instantly builds three assault rifle resources and reduces the recharge time of all your assault rifle consumers.
  • Extra Clip - [3 AP, Passive]
    Lock & Load builds two additional assault rifle resources.
  • Fire in the Hole - [4 AP, Active, Single]
    Attaches a grenade to your target which explodes for a large amount of damage. Consumes all assault rifle resources.
  • Experience (AR Ability) - [7 AP, Elite Passive]
    Increases the efficiency of all your leech effects.

Tier Two Abilities

The second tier of Assault Rifle abilities can only be accessed once the player has unlocked all of the abilities from both Engage and Support.

Take Point

  • Three Round Burst - [9 AP, Active, Burst, Single]
    A single target assault rifle burst that deals a large amount of damage over multiple shots. Consumes all assault rifle resources.
  • Call Your Shots - [12 AP, Passive]
    Whenever you successfully hit, your hit chance improves. This effect is removed on glancing hits.
  • Hip Fire - [16 AP, Active, Single]
    A short range assault rifle attack that deals a moderate amount of damage. Builds a weapon resource for each equipped weapon.
  • Steady Hip Fire - [21 AP, Passive, Modifies: Hip Fire]
    Increases the range of Hip Fire.
  • Where it Hurts - [27 AP, Active, Single]
    A single target assault rifle attack that deals a large amount of damage. Exploits afflicted state.
  • Short Controlled Bursts - [34 AP, Passive]
    Improves the hit chance of all your burst category attacks.
  • Red Mist - [50 AP, Elite Active, Single]
    A single target assault rifle attack that deals a great amount of damage, cannot be evaded and will always successfully hit.

Heavy Fire

  • Lucky Bullet - [9 AP, Passive]
    Every eighth attack you unleash will hinder the target, greatly reducing its movement speed for a short time.
  • Tactical Retreat - [12 AP, Active, Dash, AoE]
    You place an effect in a small area in front of you and dash backwards. Enemies passing through the area will have their movement speed reduced.
  • Lethality - [16 AP, Passive]
    Whenever you successfully hit, you deal increased damage.
  • Razor Wire - [21 AP, Active, Dash, AoE]
    You place an effect in a small area in front of you and dash backwards. Enemies passing through the area will have their movement speed reduced.
  • Explosives Expert - [27 AP, Passive]
    Whenever you successfully hit, you apply an explosive charge to your target that explodes for a small amount of extra damage.
  • Incendiary Grenade - [34 AP, Active, TAoE]
    You throw a grenade at your target that explodes and affects nearby enemies. The explosion deals a moderate amount of damage and damage over time. Sets afflicted state.
  • Demolition, Man - [50 AP, Elite Passive]
    Whenever you set hindered state, you also apply an explosives charge that deals a large amount of damage.

Bombardment

  • Suppressing Fire - [9 AP, Active, Frenzy, TAoE]
    A channeled assault rifle frenzy that deals a moderate amount of damage to the target and nearby enemies over multiple hits. Builds a weapon resource for each equipped weapon.
  • Sharp Shooter - [12 AP, Passive]
    Whenever you critically hit or penetrate, you get an increased hit chance for a short time.
  • Frag Grenade - [16 AP, Active, TAoE]
    An assault rifle attack that deals a large amount of damage to the target and nearby enemies. Exploits hindered state. Consumes all assault rifle resources.
  • Shoot 'Em Up - [21 AP, Passive]
    Whenever you successfully hit with a frenzy category ability, a damage over time component is added. Sets afflicted state.
  • High Explosive Grenade - [27 AP, Active, TAoE]
    An assault rifle attack that deals a large amount of damage to the target and nearby enemies. Exploits afflicted state. Consumes all assault rifle resources.
  • Accuracy - [34 AP, Passive]
    Increases the damage of all successful hits.
  • Shellshocker - [50 AP, Elite Active, Column AoE]
    A channeled assault rifle attack that deals a large amount of damage in a column in front of you. Targets caught in the fire are also slowed. Sets hindered state.

Squad Duty

  • Shot of Anima - [9 AP, Active, Direct Heal, Single, Effect: Leech]
    A single target assault rifle ability that heals for a large amount and leaves a short duration leech effect on your defensive target. Consumes all assault rifle resources
  • Increased Dosage - [12 AP, Passive, Modifies: Shot of Anima]
    The direct heal from Shot of Anima is increased, but there is no leech effect.
  • Anima Burst - [16 AP, Active, Burst, Single, Effect: Leech]
    An assault rifle burst that deals a moderate amount of damage and heals your defensive target for a small amount, plus a part of the damage done. Builds one assault rifle resource.
  • Improved Bursts - [21 AP, Passive]
    Improves the efficiency of all your burst category abilities.
  • Transfuse Anima - [27 AP, Active, Leech, Single]
    An assault rifle attack that deals a large amount of damage and heals your defensive target for part of the damage done. Exploits hindered state.
  • Shadow Medic - [34 AP, Passive]
    Improves all your direct heals.
  • Re-Animator - [50 AP, Elite Active, Single]
    A channeled heal.

Support Team

  • Leech Therapy - [9 AP, Passive]
    Your leech abilities will also heal friendly targets around you if the target is in afflicted state.
  • Energise - [12 AP, Active, Self, Effect: Increased Leech]
    Increases the damage of your leech abilities for a short duration.
  • Anticoagulant - [16 AP, Passive]
    Your leech effects also do damage over time. Sets afflicted state.
  • Platoon - [21 AP, Active, Direct Heal, Group]
    Heals your entire party for a moderate amount.
  • Post-op - [27 AP, Passive]
    All your leech abilities heal for a moderate amount when they expire.
  • Mortar Support - [34 AP, Active, Direct Heal, TAoE]
    A direct heal that heals your target and nearby friends for a moderate amount.
  • Veteran - [50 AP, Elite Passive]
    For each seventh leech effect you apply, all your group members are healed for a moderate amount and get a short duration leech effect.

Ground Support

  • Groundwork - [9 AP, Active, TAoE, Effect: Leech]
    An assault rifle attack that deals a moderate amount of damage to your target and nearby enemies. Gives nearby friends a leech effect. Builds an assault rifle resource
  • Improved Groundwork - [12 AP, Passive, Modifies: Groundwork]
    Groundwork heals nearby friends who already have a leech effect on them.
  • Anima Outbreak - [16 AP, Active, Frenzy, TAoE]
    An assault rifle frenzy attack that deals a large amount of damage to the target and nearby enemies. Nearby friends are healed for a moderate amount.
  • Outbreak Alert - [21 AP, Passive, Modifies: Anima Outbreak]
    Friendly targets within range of Anima Outbreak get a major hit chance increase if they are at almost full health.
  • Anima Vessel - [27 AP, Active, Single, Effect: Leech]
    A single target assault rifle attack that deals a moderate amount of damage and places an effect on the target that returns health to attackers. Exploits afflicted state.
  • Leeching Frenzy - [34 AP, Passive]
    All your frenzy category abilities also place an effect on the target that returns a small amount of health to attackers.
  • Reap and Sew - [50 AP, Elite Active, Group, Effect: Leech]
    Gives all group members a large leech effect for a short time.
