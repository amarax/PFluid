The skill you gain from being able to equip the rocket launcher places a GTAoE where your rocket launcher attacks hit, which deals small damage over time to enemies that remain in it.

Rocket Launcher

  • Pop Shot - [50 AP, Active Auxiliary]
    A single shot, target-centered area effect attack.
  • Rangefinder - [50 AP, Passive Auxiliary]
    Increases the range of your Rocket Launcher attacks by 5 metres.
  • Clusterstruck - [50 AP, Active Auxiliary]
    Fire a volley of rockets for a ground-targeted area effect attack.
  • Rocket Science - [50 AP, Passive Auxiliary]
    Reduces the cooldown on your Rocket Launcher abilities by 50%.
  • Death From Above - [50 AP, Active Auxiliary]
    Jump 15 metres, causing area effect damage at takeoff and landing. Using this ability inflicts damage to yourself. Don't try this at home.
  • Warhead - [50 AP, Passive Auxiliary]
    Your Rocket Launcher attacks do an additional 15% damage.
  • Big Red Button - [50 AP, Active Auxiliary]
    A highly damaging, target-centered area effect attack.
