The "Rotting Teeth" debuff mentioned in all the passives is a special DOT Afflict set by all chainsaw attacks. It cannot be cleansed by normal means, and instead must be healed through, though a certain amount of healing done in a short time will remove the debuff.

Chainsaw

  • Timber - [50 AP, Active Auxiliary, Single]
    A single target melee attack.
  • Revved Up - [50 AP, Passive Auxiliary]
    Increase the damage dealt by “Rotting Teeth” by 100%
  • Hurly-Burly - [50 AP, Active Auxiliary, PBAoE]
    A channeled PBAoE that affects up to 5 enemies in a 5 meter radius around you, hitting them every 0.5 seconds for 2.5 seconds.
  • No Love Lost - [50 AP, Passive Auxiliary]
    Improves “Rotting Teeth” to generate more hate each time it deals damage.
  • Diamond Grit - [50 AP, Active Auxiliary, Self]
    Places a reflect barrier on you that reflects 50% of the incoming damage back to the attacker and dissipates after reflecting a large amount of damage or if 10 seconds has passed. The magnitude of the barrier scales based on the Weapon Power of your Chainsaw.
  • Degradation - [50 AP, Passive Auxiliary]
    Increases the amount of healing required in order to remove the rotting teeth debuff.
  • The Carver's Art - [50 AP, Active Auxiliary, Single]
    A channeled attack that hits every 0.2 seconds for 3 seconds. Both you and your target are unable to move while this ability is active.
