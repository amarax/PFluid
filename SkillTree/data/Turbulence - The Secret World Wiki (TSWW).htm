Turbulence

From The Secret World Wiki
Jump to: navigation, search

Turbulence is one of three Miscellaneous Ability tracks available to the player immediately upon starting The Secret World.

Turbulence focuses on the ability to manipulate 'Hate' - the Secret World's unit of measurement for how much an enemy considers a player threatening. The higher an enemy's Hate for a player, the more likely they are to single that player out first before dealing with any other foes. Unlike Subversion, however, Turbulence is intended to increase Hate, rather than decrease it, drawing the enemy's attention towards the player, and encouraging them to leave weaker allies alone.

Abilities

  • Provoke - [9 AP, Active, Single]
    A single target taunt, increasing the likelihood that the target will attack you.
  • Agitator - [12 AP, Passive]
    Significantly increases the amount of hate you generate.
  • Mass Provocation - [16 AP, Active, TAoE]
    You taunt nearby targets, increasing the likelihood that they will attack you.
  • Fuel to the Fire - [21 AP, Passive]
    Every time you successfully hit, you generate extra hate.
  • Mockingbird - [27 AP, Active, Self]
    You generate increase hate for a short time, and each incoming hit will heal you for a small amount.
  • Resilient Defender - [34 AP, Passive]
    Whenever you evade or block an attack, or an attack glances you, you will be healed for a moderate amount.
  • Stoicism - [50 AP, Elite Active, Self]
    You generate a significant amount of additional hate and your mitigation is increase for a short time.
